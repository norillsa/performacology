continuous learning

We seek to empower performance testers, engineers and architects with a diversity and depth of learning opportunities. This is our first and most important objective. Our ongoing collaboration with the Workshop on Performance and Reliability community. emphasizes our committments to each other and the community of performance professionals worldwide. We will seek to offer free tutorials, webinars, podcasts and videos from the expert participants from our community.

research library

Collecting and curating the knowledge from the performance thought-leaders across our industry is our challenge. Through the unique approach to performance certification, we endeavor to make public every experience report submitted for certification. We are sharing our unique experiences in the disciplines for performance engineering. Every performance tester and engineer who takes up the challenging work should have the support of our community and the reference material to work with confidence and without fear.

open-source certification

Are are embracing concepts and processes to invent a new open-source, community-driven certification program for performance testing and engineering professionals. The program leverages the collaborative aspects of open-source software development while disrupting the traditional schemes of for-profit IT training and certification programs. The value of a Performacology certification is derived from the ever-evolving contributions of the community of our peers.
